Explore the Stars from a New Perspective

Our Mission

Providing everyone a way to expand their perspective about the stars and proliferating the beautity of astronomy to all.

Why expand your Point of View?

Exploring the stars from exoplanets offers a new perspective on our place in the universe. By immersing ourselves in the celestial views from these distant worlds, we can better appreciate the diversity and vastness of the cosmos.

Who we are

We are a group of highschoolers from various parts across the nation. TARS-1 not only allowed us to deepen our connection, but also see a common night sky. Connor Lee - CA, Jerry Yan - CA, Aldric Benalan - NJ, Andy Long - TX.
